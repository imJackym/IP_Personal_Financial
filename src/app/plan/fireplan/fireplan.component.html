INFO - There are severals ways to achieve FIRE.
expand_more expand_less
FIRE is "Financial Independence, Retire Early".
1. 4% rule
2. Whole life of total expenditure is equal or small than to current total asset.
3. total passive income is equal to or greater than total expenditure
4. when whole life of total passive income add up with current asset is equal to and greater than total expenditure.
