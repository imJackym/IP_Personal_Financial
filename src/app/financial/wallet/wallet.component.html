Wallet is categorized into 3 groups/components of different assets.

1. Cash - consider as money in liquidity which is not in stable for payment. - It is not recommended to keep too many percentages in the wallet as cash used for payment as a dead pool that does not grow and save and can easily be spent more.
2. Bank - consider as money in save for saving. - Bank is only one role that is KEEPING. Avoiding financial shortfalls in any emergency or attend any target wanted
3. Investment - consider as money in growing for future. - Investing in goals is about getting there faster. But it is not recommended to invest too much, it should be balanced with saving to provide for daily use and any emergency cases
